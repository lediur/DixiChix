What We Do

Here's a little blurb about what we are trying to do with a drone ...

Launch it from a car.
Autonomously navigate around the car.
Video record the driving aerially.
Process the video.
Integrate the solution with a demo car.

Driving enthusiasts will be able to easily capture their driving experience from an aerial perspective.
Our mission is to provide a simple way for driving enthusiasts to share thrills.

Rapid Prototyping






     

Our prototyping involved:

  • Preparation - Setting the drone up outside out of the car.
  • Pre-processing - Choosing "footage points" for the drive.
  • Driving - Changing "footage points" during the drive.
  • Drone Return - Determining how the driver will retrieve the drone.
  • Footage Transfer - Determining how the user accesses the final video.
